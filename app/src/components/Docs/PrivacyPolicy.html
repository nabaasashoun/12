Welcome to TrendSync ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Quick Summary:
  • We collect information to provide and improve our marketplace services
  • Your data is stored securely and never sold to third parties

  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, username, and password
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, location, contact details, and business information (for sellers)
  • Transaction Information: Purchase history, order details, payment information, and delivery addresses
  • Communication: Messages, reviews, ratings, and feedback you provide
  • Verification Documents: NIN number, ID photos, and business verification documents (for sellers)

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used, and interactions
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address or GPS (with your permission)
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience and remember your preferences

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: When you sign in via Google OAuth
  • Payment Processors: Payment confirmation and transaction status
  • Analytics: Google Analytics and other analytics providers

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Provide Services: Create and manage your account, process transactions, and facilitate communication
  • Improve Platform: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • Communicate: Send order confirmations, updates, and important notifications
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries, resolve disputes, and provide assistance
  • Personalization: Recommend products, tailor content, and improve recommendations
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse

2.2 For Sellers

  • Business Verification: Verify your identity and business information
  • Payment Processing: Process payouts for your sales
  • Performance Analytics: Track sales, views, and customer engagement
  • Marketing: Send promotional information about your store

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

  • Buyers: Your profile name, location, and rating are visible to sellers
  • Sellers: Your business name, location, rating, and contact information are visible to buyers
  • Reviews: Your reviews and ratings are public and visible to all users
  • Messages: Your messages are visible to the recipient

3.2 With Third-Party Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: To process transactions and payments
  • Delivery Services: For shipping and delivery of orders
  • Analytics Providers: To analyze platform usage and improve services
  • Cloud Services: For data storage and hosting
  • Communication Services: To send notifications and emails

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of TrendSync, our users, or others.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Location: Your data is stored on secure servers
  • Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
  • Deletion: You can request account deletion, and we will remove your data within 30 days

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Authentication: JWT tokens for secure API access
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access Control: Strict access controls for employees and contractors
  • Backups: Regular automated backups to prevent data loss
